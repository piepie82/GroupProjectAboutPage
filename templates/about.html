UBS Group AG

About UBS

UBS Group AG is a Swiss multinational investment bank and financial services company founded and based in Switzerland. It provides wealth management, asset management, and investment banking services to clients worldwide. With a heritage dating back to the mid-19th century, UBS is one of the largest global wealth managers and is headquartered in Zurich and Basel.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide clients with financial expertise, outstanding services, and innovative solutions. We strive to support our clients in achieving their financial goals while upholding the highest standards of integrity and ethics.

Global Presence

UBS operates in over 50 countries and employs over 70,000 people, serving both private and institutional clients. Our strong global presence is backed by in-depth local knowledge and expertise, enabling us to deliver tailored solutions across various markets and sectors.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including:

  • Wealth Management
  • Investment Banking
  • Asset Management
  • Personal and Corporate Banking
